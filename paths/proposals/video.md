# VIDEO proposal template

> The orchestrator fills this in and sends it to the human at the gate. Adapt to
> your domain. Reply verbs come from `gate:` in triage.yaml (no leading slash).

```
🎥 VIDEO proposal: <title>   (slug: <slug>)

What users struggle with
<one paragraph>

Existing solution
<what it is, where it lives, who maintains it>

Why a video helps
- Specific friction: ...
- Why existing explainers fall short: ...

Proposed video
- Working title: ...
- Runtime: ~<N> min
- Audience: ...
- Chapters:
  1. <beat>
  2. <beat>
  3. <beat>

Sources
- <link to the solution's docs>
- <link showing the confusion>

Estimate
- Spend for slides + script: ~$<X>

Reply:  approve <slug>   |   shelve <slug>: <reason>   |   modify <slug>: <change>
```
